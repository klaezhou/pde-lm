\section{Diffusion Equation }

diffusion equation:
\[ \frac{\partial y}{\partial t} = \frac{\partial^2 y}{\partial x^2} - e^{-
   t} (\sin (\pi x) - \pi^2 \sin (\pi x)), \; x \in [1, - 1], t \in [0, 1] \]
with the IC
\[ y (x, 0) = \sin (\pi x) \]
and the Dirichlet BD
\[ y (- 1, t) = y (1, t) = 0 \]
The solution is $y = e^{- t} \sin (\pi x)$
